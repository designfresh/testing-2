How We Began

Young Graduate was founded in 1997 by Dijonn Taylor. Living close to Manchester's University she saw many graduate's doning their bright cap and gowns as they made their way to have all their hard work and achievements recognised. Dijonn's vision was that every young person in her local area should have the opportunity of experiencing a graduation ceremony in recognition of their efforts and achievements. In May 2022, Dijonn was nominated by the Department of Education to attend the Queen's Garden Party at Buckingham Palace, in recognition of her services to education and children services.
